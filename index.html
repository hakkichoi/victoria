Company History

From First Trade to
Trusted Partnership

Founded on the principle of transparent intermediary trade, passnine has grown its presence across three continents since 2023.

2023

Established Brazil–China and Brazil–Korea trade corridors. First transactions in Brazilian sugar (ICUMSA45) and Chicken Paw successfully intermediated.

2024

Expanded to China–Korea corridor. Introduced VHP sugar and broadened protein product range. Purchase agency service launched for risk-free trade.

2025

Ongoing partnerships with BRF and JBS for Chicken Paw. Added other Brazilian agricultural commodities including soybean and corn.

Purchase Agency Service

Risk-Free Trade Through
Our Agency Network

Eliminate counterparty risk entirely. Our China-based partner enables domestic contract arrangements so buyers receive goods before payment, and sellers receive guaranteed settlement.

01

Buyer Contracts Locally

The buyer signs a domestic contract with our China-based partner, eliminating cross-border payment risk entirely.

02

Goods Shipped & Received

Product is shipped from Brazil and delivered. The buyer inspects and accepts the goods before any payment is made.

03

Payment After Receipt

The buyer makes payment only after confirming receipt. Our agency absorbs the settlement risk on the seller's behalf.

04

Guaranteed Settlement

Sellers receive secure, guaranteed payment through our partner network, eliminating default or delay risk.
